Project 02
ReportPilot
Vertical SaaS — Digital Marketing Agencies
◎ Designed

Digital marketing agencies spend every Friday manually writing client reports — pulling numbers from Google Ads, Meta, and GA4, writing the narrative, formatting it, sending it. Every client. Every week. By hand. Every existing tool shows them the data. None of them write the words. ReportPilot connects to their ad accounts, writes the full report in the agency's own voice — what changed, why, what to do next — and emails it to the client automatically. The agency wakes up Saturday. Their clients already have their reports. Nobody worked Friday evening.

Vertical SaaS Marketing Tech LLM Automation API Integration B2B
Problem validated through agency interviews and community research
Integrations mapped — Google Ads, Meta, GA4 APIs
Voice matching — reports generated in the agency's own tone
GTM outlined — agency Slack groups, ProductHunt, cold outreach
Project 03
ASA — Market Research
AI Observability Startup — Deep Research Sprint
◈ Research

ASA (AI System Avatar) is a conversational observability layer for AI agents. Instead of reading dashboards or log files, engineers ask in plain English: "Why did my agent fail last night?" and get a human-readable answer. Before building anything, I ran a structured 9-part research sprint covering problem validation, competitive landscape across 10 tools, market sizing, regulatory implications, customer segmentation, GTM strategy, and a full risk analysis.
